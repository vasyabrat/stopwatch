Privacy Policy — Stopwatch Games

Last updated: 21 June 2026

This policy explains what information the Stopwatch Games app collects and how it is used. The solo Stopwatch and Guess‑the‑Time modes work entirely on your device and collect no data at all. The sections below apply only when you choose to play online multiplayer (Impostor or Closest Wins).

What we collect

  • A display name you type when joining or creating an online game. You choose this — it does not need to be your real name.
  • Gameplay data needed to run a match: the game code, lobby information (host name, game type, player count), turn order, your timing results, and votes.

What we do NOT collect

  • No email address, password, phone number, or real‑world identity (sign‑in is anonymous).
  • No location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.
  • No payment information — purchases are handled entirely by Apple or Google.
  • No analytics or third‑party advertising or tracking.

How your data is stored

Online game data is stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database, which acts as our data processor. Authentication is anonymous, so no account is created. Google's handling of this data is governed by the Firebase Privacy and Security terms.

How long we keep it

Game data is temporary. A lobby and its game are deleted when the game ends or the host leaves. We do not keep a history of past games or results.

Who can see it

The display name you choose and your in‑game results are visible to the other players in your game. Please don't use a name or text you wouldn't want other players to see.

Sharing

We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties for advertising. Data is only transmitted to Google Firebase to operate the multiplayer features.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. Please do not enter personal information as your display name.

Your choices

If you prefer not to share any information, use the solo modes, which collect nothing. Choosing not to enter an online game means no data is collected.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
